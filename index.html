How to Play

  • Move: Use W A S D to walk.
  • Jump: Press Space to jump. Stronger jump helps clear gaps.
  • Look / Rotate: Hold Right Mouse Button and move mouse to rotate the camera (pointer lock).
  • Fly (Admin): Open the admin panel (top-right), click Enable Fly. While flying use Space to ascend and Shift to descend. Adjust Fly Speed slider to change speed.
  • Balloons (Admin): Use the admin panel to spawn balloons for fun.
  • Victory / Levels: Reach the final platform to win. After victory you can Next Level or Restart to go home.
  • Restart Home: From the Victory screen click Restart to return to the home menu.
